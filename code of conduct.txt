Code of conduct

Attend all scheduled meetings, whether in-person or virtual, unless there are unavoidable circumstances. Notify the team in advance if you're unable to attend and catch up on missed information promptly. Most important meeting being 12-2 Thursday lab slot

Treat all members equally and allow each person in team to be heard.

Do not use any language that may be deemed offensive

Fulfil tasks promptly and to the best of your abilities.

When giving feedback do so in a constructive manner and be open to receiving feedback from all team members

Work together on tasks and be willing to share work and ideas across the group

When attending meetings online or in person do not be under the influence of alcohol or drugs
